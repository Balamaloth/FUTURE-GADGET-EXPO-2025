Frequently Asked Questions

When will tickets go on sale?

Early bird tickets are available now until January 31st, 2025. Regular pricing begins February 1st.

What is your refund policy?

We offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person or rolled over to next years event.

Are there group discounts available?

Yes! Groups of 10+ receive a 15% discount, and groups of 25+ receive 25%. Contact us for corporate packages.

Can I exhibit at the event?

Absolutely! We have exhibition packages available. Contact our sponsorship team for details.

Is the venue accessible?

The Moscone Center is fully accessible with wheelchair access, restrooms, and reserved seating.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all current health guidelines, including sanitization, ventilation, and optional mask requirements.
