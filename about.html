About Future Gadget Expo

Connecting the world’s brightest minds to shape tomorrow’s technology

Our Mission

Future Gadget Expo is the world’s premier technology exhibition, bringing together innovators, entrepreneurs, investors, and enthusiasts exploring the cutting-edge technologies that will define our future.

Since our inception in 2020, we have been at the forefront of showcasing breakthrough innovations in artificial intelligence, robotics, space technology, biotechnology, and sustainable tech solutions.

Event Location

Moscone Convention Center

Address: 747 Howard Street, San Francisco, CA 94103

The Moscone Center is San Francisco’s premier event venue, centrally located and offering state-of-the-art facilities for large conferences.

Getting There:
  • BART: Montgomery St. or Powell St. stations (10-minute walk)
  • Muni: Multiple bus lines serve the area
  • Parking: Several garages nearby
  • Airport: 30-minute drive from SFO
